About Us

Quick Transport and Logistics Solutions

Dropu Couriers is an emerging player in the couriers and logistics industry. Established to address a specific gap in Kenya's road freight market, Dropu Couriers specializes in transporting documents, parcels, and small packages within a 100-kilometer radius of Nairobi CBD. We cater to local businesses, individuals, and corporate offices requiring reliable, same-day delivery services not offered by larger providers like DHL, FedEx, and UPS. Our services are designed to be reliable, timely, efficient, and effective, targeting SMEs, corporates, and individuals within the CBD.

RELIABLE

We are committed to consistently delivering on our promises, ensuring that every package reaches its destination safely and on time. Our clients can trust us to handle their deliveries with the utmost care and dependability.

INNOVATIVE

We embrace cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions to streamline our operations and enhance the customer experience. Our commitment to innovation drives us to continuously improve and stay ahead in the logistics industry
